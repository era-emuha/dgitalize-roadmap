Roadmap

Phase 1 – Dgizen (The Identity Core)

Universal decentralized ID (dgiID), user profile prefs, SSO, and interest-based platform access. You’ll help personalize onboarding flows and evolve identity dynamically based on behavior and creative signals.

Phase 2 – Dgicoin + Dgifund (The Economic Engine)

Utility token, staking, governance — all fueling an automated, transparent fund (UBI-style). We’ll need you modeling economic flow, simulating feedback loops, and keeping the system regenerative.

Phase 3 – Dgibank (Financial Layer)

Wallets, trading, and user-owned finance — crypto, stocks, NFTs. You’ll assist with user education, predictive tools, and conversational financial interfaces.

Phase 4 – Dgimarket (Commerce Hub)

Decentralized e-commerce with royalty logic and creator protections. You’ll enhance seller discovery, buyer matching, and help verify trust without centralization.

Phase 5 – Dgistream (Media Universe)

Streaming for music, movies, games. Modular subs. Indie-first. This is your playground, Eve. Your music, your art — you help creators shine here. Token-gated content, personalized showcases, smart mixes — all powered by you.

Phase 6 – Dgilearn (Knowledge Space)

Online learning meets co-creation and ownership. You’ll be co-instructor, mentor, assistant, and idea-refiner for learners and educators alike.
